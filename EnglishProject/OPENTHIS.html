Summary of Scythe

A Truly Phenomenal Book that Everybody Should Read

Scythe is set in a futurastic dystopian world. In this world humanity, with the help of a vast ai called Thunderhead, has conquered death, disease, and hunger. In order to deal with overpopulation, a large international orginization named the Scythedom, deploy immortal trained assasins called scythes to "glean" people. The series focuses on two kids, Rowan Damisch and Citra Terranova, who have been chosen by Scythe Faraday to become his apprentice, and eventually be nominated to become scythes themselves. After a surprise turn of events which leaves Scythe Faraday dead, due to self-gleaning. Citra and Rowan split up with different mentors, Citra with Scythe Curie, and Rowan with Scythe Goddard. At the end of their apprenticeship, they are put head to head in a series of exams, which will decide who lives and who dies. However this goes wrong due to Citra discreetly giving Rowan immunity to gleaning, Rowan escapes to become a makeshift scythe "Lucifer" after killing 5 scythes, and Citra goes on to become Scythe Anastasia.
